Hi, I'm Patricia Tse!

A senior at Lick-Wilmerding High School and Computer Scientist

San Franciscan. Student. Foodie. Guitarist. Pianist. Pun-lover. Arist.

Having been born and raised in San-Francisco, I have always known about the tech bubble. Since I was very young, I knew that I wanted to become a scientist, engineer, or artist since I loved using my hands and creating things. It wasn't until 8th grade/freshman year that I realized that I wanted to be computer scientist.

To me, computer science is a way for me to come with solutions to issues that plaque communities, whether big or large. The thing that draws me most to computer science, is that I can be part of the creative process of coming up with an idea, designing, programming, and testing.

While I love coding, I also enjoy countless other hobbies such as art (digital and traditional), animating, photography, basketball, volleyball, swimming, guitar, piano, composing music, and video editing.
