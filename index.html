Hi, I'm Patricia Tse!


Student. San Franciscan. Hobby Artist. Lover of Reese's Peanut Butter Cups. Computer scientist.


Having been born and raised in the San Francisco Bay Area, I have always known about the tech bubble. Since I was very young, I knew that I wanted to become a scientist, engineer, or artist since I loved creating things. It wasn't until 8th grade/freshman year that I was first exposed to computer science and realized that that was what I wanted to pursue.



To me, computer science is a way for me to come with solutions to issues that are prevalent in our communities, whether big or small. The thing that draws me most to computer science, is that I can be part of every step of the creative process -- starting from the ideation to the designing, programming, and eventually, the testing.


While I love coding, I also enjoy countless other hobbies such as art, skating, sports, listening + playing music, and Minecraft.
