A BIT ABOUT ME :)

Software Engineer

Hi! I'm Patricia, a UC Berkeley computer science grad with experience in backend and fullstack development. I've spent two summers interning with Amazon's Alexa Shopping team and am currently a fullstack developer for Sotira.

Since I was young, I wanted to become a scientist, engineer, or artist since I loved ideating and creating things. It wasn't until highschool that I was first exposed to computer science and realized that that was something I wanted to pursue. To me, computer science is a way to come with solutions to issues that are prevalent in our communities, whether big or small. The thing that draws me most to computer science, is that I can be part of every step of the creative process -- starting from the ideation to the designing, programming, and eventually, the testing.

While I love coding, I also enjoy other hobbies like art, skating, sports, music, and going on hikes/food runs with friends.
