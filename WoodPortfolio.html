Wooden Phone Holder


    With a partner, we were assigned a project to make a phone holder than fit within a 8x8x8 box, was less than one pound, is functional, and uses at least 2 CNC machines and 2 materials.

    We began the project by brainstorming ideas. After choosing an idea, we began building. Using the 3d printer, we created a hat. Using the CNC, we cut out the wood base. Lastly, we used the laser cutter to cut out teeth. We then glued them all together.
