Project information

  • Role: Committee Member
  • Dates: 2019 - 2020
  • Skillset: Adobe XD, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Lightroom

The Media and Marketing Committee is responsible for facilitating most avenues of communication between other committees and members of the Regents'​ and Chancellor's Scholars Association. We were able to apply our knowledge of Adobe software and DSLR photography, to regularly create promotional material for RCSA.

As a member, my role included:

  • Designing banners, logos, and merchandise using Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe InDesign, serving 800+ scholars
  • photographing and edited pictures of various RCSA events using Adobe products
  • Copy-editing all promotional announcements before being released to social media platforms
  • redesigning Inter-UC Conference 2020 website, serving Regents' members from all 9 UCs
