Year in Review

Overview of expenses breakdown over the year.
Month by month graph of specific expense categories.

Project information

  • Dates: Winter 2024 - Present
  • Skillset: React, Javascript, HTML, CSS

My yearly spending in review website parses CSV files and utilizes React to display statistics about my spending patterns for the year. This is similar to my year in review project but instead of habits and sleep patterns, I look only at spending habits. Throughout the year, I keep track of my spending using Notion. At the end of the year, I export my data as a CSV file and use my website to cleanly summarize the data.
