Hyphen-Hacks Hackathon

Head of Graphic Design (2017 - 2018)

Hyphen-Hacks is an annual twenty-four hour hackathon for high school students across the Bay Area that takes place at Lick-Wilmerding High School. In 2017, me and a group of high schoolers planned, fundraised, and organized the first student-run hackathon at Lick-Wilmerding High School. We were able to serve 200+ attendees, partnering with 10+ sponsors.

My role as head of graphic design included:

  • designing logo and stickers using Adobe Photoshop, designing t-shirts and hoodies using Adobe Illustrator, and creating maps and posters for the event using Adobe Photoshop
  • reach out to potential sponsors and fundraising
  • Facilitate the actual 24-hour event, helping with catering, set-up and clean-up
