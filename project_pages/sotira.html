Convergent - Sotira

Full-Stack Web Developer (2020 - Present)

Convergent is an interdisciplinary entrepreneurship club whose goal is to develop members' learning and understanding of product development in our members through semester-long collaborative projects. My group and I were in the Convergent Accelerator Program during the majority of our development stage for Sotira.

Sotira is a site built by UC Berkeley students to help crowdsoruce algorithms for specific Berkeley classes. These algorithms range from advanced mathematics to graph search and can be coded and submitted directly through the site.

My roles included:

  • creating the frontend of our site using HTML and CSS
  • writing algorithms for classes including algorithms like Chinese Reminader Theorem, floating pointer number conversion, generative truth tables, etc.
  • collaborating with group members to implement a working debugger and IDE directly onto the website
