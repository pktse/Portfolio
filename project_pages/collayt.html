Convergent is an interdisciplinary entrepreneurship club whose goal is to develop members' learning and understanding of product development in our members through semester-long collaborative projects. Collayt is a site and platform built by UC Berkeley students to help connect students (and alumni) with others through academic opportunities such as side projects and study groups.

Front-End Web Developer:

With a team of 2 other people, I helped create the front-end of our site. Using HTML and CSS, we coded the website, following the UI/UX that I had designed.

UI/UX design:

As the only UI/UX designer of the team, I was in charge of designing the UI/UX of the website using Adobe XD. A big part of designing was the communication with the rest of the team, having to prepare presentations on the UI/UX designs. In addition, I was also responsible for creating and running own UI/UX tests, collecting feedback from users, consolidating and presenting results to the team.

Graphic designer:

Being the only graphic designer, I was in charge of creating banners, artwork, image place holders, and error pages using Procreate. I also designed the logo using Adobe Illustrator and Procreate.
