MeshNotes

Project information

  • Dates: Spring 2023 - Present
  • Skillset: Java
Check out my design doc!

The idea behind meshNotes is to have a more graph structured way of storing notes. I always feel like I have many thoughts that I want to jot down on paper that end up being closely related to previous jotted thoughts. In general, notes are stored chronologically or in groups. I felt like it would be really cool if there was a structure where we could visually see how different thoughts/notes/entries are related or connected to each other.
