?

Project information

  • Dates: Spring 2023 - Present
  • Skillset: Python, Pandas

Using police incident reports data from DataSF , I wanted to better understand what's happening in my city. I personally often use MUNI to get around the city, and often have to consider both time efficiency and safety of each of the routes I consider. I was curious if there might be a trend or pattern I could isolate, so I utilized Pandas and Python to analyze SF's public police incident data. I also created a model that would try to predict the type of incident given the place and time of the origin of the call.
