Play our game!
For our client project, we were tasked to create a website for another person. My client wanted a website dedicated to her favorite kpop band.

Project information

  • Dates: Summer 2018
  • Skillset: Scratch, Python, Arduino, web development, data analysis

Girls Who Code is a non-profit organization that focuses on closing the gender gap in the Tech field. Students who participate in the Summer Immersion Program (SIP), learns about coding and technology over the course of 7 weeks, covering a new topic each week.

As a member, my role included:

  • Designing banners, logos, and merchandise using Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe InDesign, serving 800+ scholars
  • photographing and edited pictures of various RCSA events using Adobe products
  • Copy-editing all promotional announcements before being released to social media platforms
  • redesigning Inter-UC Conference 2020 website, serving Regents' members from all 9 UCs
