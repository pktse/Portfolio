Phi Beta Lambda

Tech Fall Analyst (Fall 2019)

Regents'​ and Chancellor's Scholars Association (RCSA) is dedicated to helping Regents’ and Chancellor’s Scholarship recipients of UC Berkeley develop and grow, hosting numerous events, like faculty game night and alumni dinners, and providing resources, ranging from mentorship programs to resume assistance.

During my time in RCSA, I was a part of the Media and Marketing Committee (M&M). The committee is responsible for facilitating most avenues of communication between other committees and members of the association. We were able to apply our marketable skills, including knowledge of Adobe software (Illustrator, InDesign, Lightroom) and DSLR photography, to regularly create promotional material for RCSA. Through my experience with the committee, I was able to train and develop my eyes for design.

As a member, my role included:

  • Designing banners, logos, and merchandise using Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe InDesign, serving 800+ scholars
  • photographing and edited pictures of various RCSA events using Adobe products
  • Copy-editing all promotional announcements before being released to social media platforms
  • redesigning Inter-UC Conference 2020 website, serving Regents' members from all 9 UCs
