Phi Beta Lambda

Tech Fall Analyst (Fall 2019)

Berkeley Phi Beta Lambda (PBL) is a chapter of Future Business Leaders of America-Phi Beta Lambda, the largest student business organization in the nation. Berkeley PBL helps members build the necessary technical and interpersonal skillset to succeed in wide range of business pursuits and intersections. Within the club, there are multiple committes ranging from Client Teams to the Technology committee, to the Finance committee.

The Tech Committee focuses on bridging the gap between software and business, providing a foundational understanding of how technology works within corporations. Through assignments, members are able to develop their programming skills and apply it to professional settings.

As a fall analyst, my role included:

  • Using Python and Panda to analyze Facebook data and APIs and draw correlations between usage and user
  • applying my knowledge of Panda to machine learning, creating an algorithm that predicts real estate prices
  • creating an algorithm that would generate an optimal table of time slots based on the preferences of members which was used by 65+ members
  • using Selenium and web scraping to automate the process of booking library study rooms
